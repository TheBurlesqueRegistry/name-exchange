The Burlesque Registry

Welcome to the Burlesque Registry! This is a site where you can register your performer name, a burlesque school, or festival for others to find and reference. The Burlesque Registry does not arbitrate who owns names, nor claims any ownership to them. This site is simply a repository to help performers find new and unique names along with providing resources to find places to grow.

Performers

Here's where you can register your stage name and associate it with any troupe(s). Feel free to add links to your socials, performer photos, and let us know who you are.

Shows

Register any upcoming shows here! Add a poster image, your cast, venue and time. If your cast has already registered with us, we'll highlight it in their profile!

Educators

From Burlesque Schools to individual tutors, if you're teaching the art or any skills related to it, register here so people can find new resources to learn from.

Festivals

Do you run a Festival? Register it here so we know when we can submit and when we can buy tickets to cheer on our friends. We'd love to know to help support you.
